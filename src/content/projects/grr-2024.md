Gold Rush Robotics competed in the **IEEE Southeastcon 2024 Hardware Competition** in Atlanta, Georgia.

## Custom PCB Design

The headline engineering work this year was a custom PCB I designed in KiCAD that served as the robot's central nervous system. The board connected the raspberry pi to all of the motors and sensors

the board handled:
- Power managment
- I2C breakout
- serial communication with Roboclaw motor drivers

## From Competition to ROSCon

The desire for a better transport layer for this robot became the basis for a talk at **ROSCon 2024** in Odense, Denmark — demonstrating Micro-ROS running over CAN-FD as a general-purpose transport for embedded ROS2 nodes.
