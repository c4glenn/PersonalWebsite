**Mining Mayhem** is a robotics hardware competition I designed and ran, featured at two IEEE events:

- **IEEE Southeastcon 2025** — Charlotte, NC
- **IEEE Region 5 Annual Conference 2026** — Boulder, CO

## The Game

Teams design and build an autonomous robot to navigate a mining-themed arena, collecting and depositing game pieces under strict size, weight, and power constraints. The game is designed to test autonomous navigation, manipulation, and robust mechanical design within a competition time limit.

## My Role

As Hardware Chair for IEEE Region 5 and a member of the IEEE Southeast Conference Student Activities Committee, I was responsible for:

- Designing the game rules, field layout, and scoring system
- Fabricating and assembling the competition field
- Running the competition at both events and adjudicating matches
- Coordinating with hosting institutions and student teams across the region
