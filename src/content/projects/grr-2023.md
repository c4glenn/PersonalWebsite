Gold Rush Robotics competed in the **IEEE Southeastcon 2023 Hardware Competition** in Orlando, Florida.

The IEEE Hardware Competition is an annual challenge where teams design and build an autonomous robot to complete a specific game task under strict size, weight, and power constraints. Each year the game changes, requiring teams to design and build a new robot from scratch.

## My Role

As a core team member in my first year, I contributed to electrical design, firmware development, and robot assembly in the lead-up to competition.
